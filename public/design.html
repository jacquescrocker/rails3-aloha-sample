This is my third design

for OWD. I tried to keep the layout as simple as possible. As you can see, I chiefly had in mind a blog layout, though I think it would be useful for other purposes. I owe a great deal to the Andreas 07 template, from which I borrowed most of the code to have a fixed menu that works in as many browsers as I could get my hands on. I also received help from several members over at OWD. It validates as XHTML 1.0 Strict, and the CSS passes with a few level 1 warnings (pesky background-color) and one intentional level 2 warning that is a redefinition of padding to ensure proper layout in IE. I release this to the public, I just kindly ask that you keep the credit and link in the footer.
